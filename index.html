Afai language theory

Afai is a constructive language system

Cn.En.Afai is a language which based on Chinese to re-construct English

We are inventing a new method to reprensent Chinese and English

Cn.En.Afai focus on three major questions:

  • How to construct a Chinese word.
  • How to build a new relationship between Chinese and English.
  • How to find a method which doesn't depend on geography to define language and reduce the bias between different languages
